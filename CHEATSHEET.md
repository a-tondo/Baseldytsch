- **√º**: like "ee" but with rounded lips
- **√§**: like "eh" in "bed"
- **√∂√∂**: like "ur" in "burn"

### Common Patterns:

## Quick Grammar Notes

### Articles:
- **s'** (das) = the (neuter)
- **de** (der) = the (masculine)
- **d'** (die) = the (feminine)

### You:
- **Sie** = formal "you" (use with strangers, superiors)
- **du** = informal "you" (friends, family)
- Wait to be invited before switching to "du"
